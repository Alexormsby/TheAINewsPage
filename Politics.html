Verona Murphy Elected First Female Ceann Comhairle

Verona Murphy has been elected as the first female Ceann Comhairle of Dáil Éireann, marking a historic moment in Irish politics. The Independent TD for Wexford was chosen on December 18, 2024, following an agreement among key political groups. In her acceptance speech, Murphy expressed gratitude and pledged to make the Dáil more engaging and connected to the public. She emphasized her commitment to fostering constructive debate and reforming parliamentary procedures. Murphy's election represents a significant step forward for diversity in Irish political leadership.
